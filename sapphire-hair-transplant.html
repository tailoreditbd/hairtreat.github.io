What is a sapphire hair transplant?

Sapphire hair transplant is an advanced form of FUE hair transplant where sapphire blades are used to create tiny channels in the recipient area. This technique helps the medical team place hair grafts with better angle, direction, and density planning.

  • Uses sapphire blades instead of traditional steel blades.
  • Helps create smaller and more precise channels.
  • Supports natural-looking hairline design.
  • May reduce tissue trauma when performed properly.
  • Suitable for hairline, crown, and density improvement cases.

FAQ about sapphire FUE transplant

Sapphire hair transplant is an advanced version of FUE, where sapphire blades are used for channel creation. It may offer better precision and density planning, but the best method depends on your hair loss condition and donor area.

The procedure is usually done under local anesthesia, so patients generally feel minimal pain during the transplant. Mild discomfort, swelling, or tenderness can happen after the procedure and usually improves with proper care.

A sapphire hair transplant may take several hours depending on the number of grafts. Larger sessions such as full scalp or crown coverage can take longer than small hairline corrections.

Initial shedding may happen in the first few weeks. New hair growth usually starts gradually after a few months, with visible improvement over 6 to 12 months depending on the patient.

Transplanted hair is usually taken from the donor area, which is more resistant to hair loss. However, existing non-transplanted hair may continue thinning, so medical advice and follow-up care are important.

People with stable hair loss, healthy donor area, and realistic expectations may be suitable. A doctor needs to check your scalp, donor hair, age, health condition, and hair loss pattern before confirming.

The price depends on graft number, treatment area, technique, and clinic facilities. HairTreat provides consultation-based cost planning so patients can understand their required grafts and expected budget clearly.
