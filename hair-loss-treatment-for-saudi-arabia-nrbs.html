FAQ about hair loss treatment

The best treatment depends on the cause of hair loss, hair fall level, scalp condition, donor hair quality, and age. Common options include medical treatment, PRP therapy, scalp care, and hair transplant for advanced baldness.

Yes, hair transplant can be suitable for Saudi Arabian NRBs if they have stable hair loss and enough donor hair. A consultation helps confirm graft requirements, technique, cost, and recovery plan.

Most patients need a few days for consultation, procedure, and initial follow-up. However, the exact timeline depends on the treatment plan, graft number, and doctor's advice.

Hair transplant is usually performed under local anesthesia, so major pain is not expected during the procedure. Mild discomfort, swelling, or sensitivity may happen during recovery.

Hair loss treatment cost depends on the service type, number of sessions, graft requirement, hair loss stage, and selected package. A consultation gives the most accurate cost idea.

PRP may help support hair strength, scalp health, and early-stage hair thinning in selected patients. It is often recommended after proper diagnosis and may be combined with other treatments.
