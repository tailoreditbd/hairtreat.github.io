Our Treatments

Our Available hair treatment in Bangladesh

Our skin, hair, and laser treatment services are designed to help you look and feel your best with safe, effective, and personalized care. Using modern technology and experienced professionals, we provide solutions tailored to your unique beauty and wellness needs.

Hair PRP

PRP therapy for hair growth that helps strengthen weak hair follicles.

Hair Transplant

Advanced follicle transplant method for natural and permanent hair restoration.

Eyebrow Transplant

Precision eyebrow restoration for fuller and natural-looking brows.

Female Hair Transplant

Designed for female hair loss to improve density naturally.

Hair Line Correction

Reshapes and restores the hairline for a balanced, natural appearance

Beard Transplant

Restores beard hair density and supports natural beard growth.

FUE

Minimally invasive hair transplant method for natural-looking results.

FUT Hair Transplant

High graft-yield technique, effective for larger bald areas.

DHI Hair Transplant
