Transparent Pricing

Hair transplant cost in Bangladesh

Hair transplant cost in Bangladesh depends on the patient's hair loss level, graft requirement, technique, and treatment plan. At HairTreat, we provide consultation-based pricing so every patient can understand the expected cost clearly before starting treatment.

Factors Determining Cost:

  • Number of grafts required: The total cost usually depends on how many grafts are needed to cover the thinning or bald area properly.
  • Hair transplant technique: FUE, Sapphire FUE, and other advanced techniques may have different pricing based on precision, tools, and procedure complexity.
  • Baldness level and coverage area: Small hairline correction costs less than full crown coverage or advanced baldness restoration.
  • Donor area condition: A strong donor area helps with better graft planning, while weak donor hair may require more careful treatment planning.
  • Additional treatment support: PRP, post-transplant care, medication guidance, and follow-up support may also affect the overall treatment package.
