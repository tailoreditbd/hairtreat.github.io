How Laser Treatment Works

Stimulate Follicles

Light energy triggers cellular activity in dormant follicles, shifting them from the resting phase back to the growth phase.

Increase Blood Flow

Enhanced microcirculation delivers essential nutrients and oxygen directly to the scalp's roots for healthier growth.

Cellular Repair

Photobiomodulation reduces inflammation and repairs damaged cells, creating the optimal environment for hair.
