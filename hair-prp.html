Advanced Hair PRP Therapy

Non-surgical hair restoration using Platelet-Rich Plasma technology

What is Hair PRP Therapy?

Hair PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy is a non-surgical treatment that uses your own blood's growth factors to stimulate hair follicles and promote new hair growth. This cutting-edge treatment is ideal for addressing early-stage hair loss and thinning.

How Does It Work?

During the procedure, we draw a small amount of your blood and process it to concentrate the platelets. These platelets contain growth factors that are then injected into areas of hair loss to rejuvenate dormant hair follicles and stimulate new hair growth.

Benefits

  • Non-invasive and completely safe
  • Uses your own biological materials
  • Minimal downtime
  • Natural-looking results
  • No side effects
  • Multiple sessions for optimal results

Treatment Sessions

Typically, we recommend 4-6 sessions spaced 4-6 weeks apart for best results. Many patients see visible improvement after 2-3 sessions.
