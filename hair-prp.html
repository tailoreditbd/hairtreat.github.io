Revitalize Your Hair Follicles with Plasma Rich Platelets

PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy is a three-step medical treatment in which a person's blood is drawn, processed, and then injected into the scalp. It triggers natural hair growth and maintains it by increasing blood supply to the hair follicle.

Expert Medical Team

Uses your own blood, eliminating risk of allergic reactions.

100% Natural Procedure

Uses your own blood, eliminating risk of allergic reactions.

Quick & Minimal Downtime

The procedure takes about 45-60 minutes with no recovery time.

Is Hair PRP Right for You?

PRP therapy works best for patients in the early stages of hair loss. It is an ideal solution for:

  • Men and women with androgenic alopecia (pattern baldness).
  • Individuals experiencing hair thinning or excessive shedding.
  • Post-hair transplant patients wanting to boost graft survival.
  • Anyone looking for a non-surgical alternative to restoration.

Note for Success:

Results typically become visible after 3-4 sessions spaced one month apart. Optimal results are seen at the 6-month mark.

How PRP Works

A systematic approach to hair restoration that ensures maximum efficacy and patient comfort.

Blood Collection

Approximately 20-30ml of blood is drawn from your arm, similar to a routine blood test.

Centrifugation

The blood is processed in a centrifuge to isolate the platelet-rich plasma.

Injection

Concentrated growth factors are carefully injected into the areas of the scalp needing treatment.
