What is an Eyebrow Transplant?

Natural Growth

Using your own hair follicles ensures a 100% natural look that blends seamlessly with existing hair.

Permanent Results

Transplanted hairs are resistant to hair loss, providing a lifelong solution for thin eyebrows.

Expert Surgeons

Our specialists have performed thousands of successful FUE transplants with artistic precision.

The Procedure Path

A step-by-step journey to your perfect brows

1) Consultation

Mapping your desired shape and checking hair density.

2) Extraction

Gentle FUE harvesting from the donor site (usually nape).

3) Implantation

Precise placement of follicles following natural growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Initial healing takes about 5-7 days. Redness usually fades within the first week, and you can resume most normal activities within 48 hours.

We use local anesthesia to ensure you are comfortable throughout. Most patients report feeling only a slight pressure during the process.

Transplanted hairs typically shed within 2-3 weeks. New growth begins at 3-4 months, with full density achieved around 10-12 months.
