Cost Factors

What affects hair transplant cost?

Hair transplant pricing is not the same for every patient. The cost changes depending on how much coverage is needed and which technique gives the best result for your condition. Here are some key points:

  • Number of grafts required for coverage
  • Hair loss stage and baldness pattern
  • Donor area strength and hair density
  • Selected technique such as FUE, Sapphire FUE, or DHI
  • Treatment package, aftercare, and follow-up support
