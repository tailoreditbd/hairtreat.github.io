What is a Moustache Transplant?

Permanent Results

Transplanted follicles are resistant to hair loss and will grow for a lifetime.

Maximum Precision

Micro-incisions ensure the hair grows at the exact angle needed for realism.

Natural Aesthetics

Custom-designed patterns to match your facial structure and goals.

The Procedure Path

Our streamlined process ensures comfort and excellence from the first consultation to your final results.

Consultation

Personalized mapping and design of your ideal moustache shape.

Extraction

Gentle removal of healthy follicles from the donor area (usually the back of the scalp).

Implantation

Meticulous placement of grafts into the moustache area at the correct depth.
