Got questions? we've got answers!

We use a powerful local numbing agent to ensure the procedure is as comfortable as possible. Most patients report only minimal discomfort or a slight pressure sensation.

While some men notice immediate improvements, the full regenerative effects typically peak between 3 to 6 months after the procedure as new tissue grows.

There is virtually no downtime. Most patients can return to work and normal activities immediately, though we recommend waiting 24-48 hours before sexual activity.

Because the P-Shot uses your own blood, the risk of an allergic reaction or rejection is non-existent. Temporary swelling or minor bruising at the injection site is possible.
