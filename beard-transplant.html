What is a Beard Transplant?

Follicle Extraction

Healthy hair follicles are carefully taken from the donor area, usually the back of the scalp, using advanced FUE or FUT techniques.

Precise Beard Placement

Each follicle is meticulously implanted into the beard area to fill patchy spots or create a fuller, natural-looking beard.

Natural Growth Results

The follicles grow just like natural facial hair, matching the angle and direction for a dense, well-shaped beard.

The Procedure Path

Simple, effective, and professional steps to your wellness journey.

1) Consultation

Design your ideal beard line and assess donor hair health.

2) Extraction

Minimally invasive removal of individual follicles from the donor area.

3) Implantation

Precise placement of hairs into the beard region for density and shape.

Frequently Asked Questions

Most patients return to work within 2-3 days. The redness in the recipient area usually subsides within a week, and small scabs will fall off within 7-10 days.

Initial growth starts within 3-4 months. Significant density improvements are visible at 6 months, with the final, permanent result achieved at 12-14 months.

The procedure is performed under local anesthesia, so you will not feel pain during the surgery. Some mild soreness may occur post-op, which is easily managed with standard pain relief.
