Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, laser hair removal is widely considered a permanent reduction treatment. While “permanent” means most hair won’t grow back, some fine hairs might return over years due to hormonal changes, requiring occasional maintenance sessions.

At HairTreat, we carefully assess hair loss patterns, donor area quality, scalp condition, and future hair loss before creating a treatment plan. This ensures natural-looking results that remain balanced over time.

For optimal results, we generally recommend 6 to 8 sessions spaced 4 to 6 weeks apart. This ensures we catch hair follicles in their active growth phase.
