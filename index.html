Hair Transplant FAQ

FAQ about hair transplant in Bangladesh

Hair transplant cost in Bangladesh depends on the number of grafts, technique, baldness level, donor area condition, and treatment package. A consultation is needed to estimate the exact cost.

Hair transplant is considered a long-term hair restoration solution because healthy follicles are taken from the donor area and implanted into thinning or bald areas. However, existing non-transplanted hair may still need care.

Both FUE and Sapphire FUE are popular techniques. Sapphire FUE uses sapphire blades for channel creation and may offer more precision in suitable cases. The best option depends on your scalp condition, graft requirement, and doctor's advice.

The number of grafts depends on your hair loss level, desired density, donor area strength, and treatment goal. Some patients may need 1000–1500 grafts, while others may need 2500–4500 grafts or more.

Hair transplant is usually performed under local anesthesia, so patients generally feel minimal discomfort during the procedure. Mild soreness or tightness may happen after the treatment and is managed with proper aftercare.

Initial shedding can happen after the procedure. New hair growth usually starts gradually within a few months, while visible improvement often appears over 6 to 12 months. Final results may take longer depending on the patient.

Yes, many NRBs and overseas Bangladeshis choose hair transplant in Bangladesh due to convenience, affordability, and access to professional treatment. HairTreat BD helps overseas patients plan their consultation, procedure, and follow-up support.
