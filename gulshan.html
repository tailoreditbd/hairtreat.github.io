Transparent Pricing

Hair transplant cost in Bangladesh

Hair transplant cost in Bangladesh depends on your hair loss condition, required grafts. At HairTreat, we provide consultation-based pricing so patients can understand the cost clearly before starting the procedure.

Factors Determining Cost:

  • The number of grafts needed for your hairline, crown, or bald area plays a major role in the total cost.
  • Mild hair thinning usually needs fewer grafts, while advanced baldness may require a larger treatment plan.
  • FUE, Sapphire FUE, and other advanced options may have different pricing based on tools, precision, and procedure complexity.
  • A healthy donor area helps with better graft planning, while weak donor hair may require more careful assessment.
  • PRP support, medication guidance, follow-up care, and post-transplant support can affect the overall package price.
