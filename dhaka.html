Transparent Pricing

Average hair treatment cost in Bangladesh

Average hair treatment cost in Bangladesh depends on the type of treatment, patient condition, treatment area, number of sessions, required procedure, and aftercare support. A simple consultation or scalp treatment will not cost the same as Hair PRP, laser removal, or hair transplant because each service has a different process and requirement.

Common factors that affect hair treatment cost include:

  • Treatment type: Hair PRP, hair transplant, laser removal, scalp treatment, and personal wellness treatments have different pricing structures.
  • Patient condition: Mild hair thinning may need a smaller treatment plan, while advanced baldness or long-term hair loss may require more detailed care.
  • Number of sessions: Some treatments may need one procedure, while others may require multiple sessions for better support.
  • Technique and equipment: Advanced procedures such as Sapphire FUE, PRP, or laser-based treatment may have different costs depending on the method used.
  • Aftercare and follow-up: Medication guidance, washing instructions, recovery support, and follow-up consultation may be included in the overall treatment package.
