Take the first step toward thicker, healthier hair and renewed confidence today. Consult with us now — book your personalized appointment and get expert advice tailored just for you!

Prices vary based on key factors such as:

  • The clinic's expertise and reputation — Choose experienced specialists using modern, safe technology for the best outcomes.
  • Your specific hair and scalp condition — Mild thinning may need fewer sessions, while advanced hair loss requires a tailored plan.
  • Treatment type — Popular non-surgical options like PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy often 3–6 sessions recommended for optimal regrowth, while laser hair restoration or combined therapies fall in similar or slightly higher ranges depending on the protocol.
